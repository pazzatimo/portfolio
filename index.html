Who I Am

Practitioner. Innovator.
Community builder.

"The real power of data lies not in the numbers themselves, but in what happens when they reach the right hands at the right time."

Based in Morogoro, Tanzania, I am a versatile Data, M&E and ICT practitioner with over six years of hands on experience across health, governance, WASH and community development. I hold a Bachelor of Education in Information Communication Technology and carry multiple professional certifications in MEAL, Research Ethics, Project Management and Environmental Management.

I combine strong technical skills with real understanding of the community. I have designed and deployed MEAL systems for multi-partner programmes spanning 179 communities in three regions, trained over 1,000 students and 500+ youth on accountability and WASH, and mobilised awareness events reaching more than 10,000 community members. At the same time, I create responsive websites, write professional reports, and build ICT solutions for organisations, businesses, and individuals.

I coordinate easily with local government authorities, international NGOs and grassroots communities and I bring that same fluency to managing data, dashboards, toolkits and campaigns.
